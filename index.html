How we work

A structured, low-drama migration process.

Every engagement follows a clearly documented lifecycle—from discovery to handoff—so stakeholders know where things stand and operations teams aren’t left guessing.

1
Discovery & Assessment

Inventory identity, messaging, and security dependencies; understand current risks; align with business timelines and regulatory requirements.

2
Architecture & Planning

Design the target state, coexistence model, and migration waves. Select appropriate tooling and define rollback and communication plans.

3
Execution & Validation

Run pilots, refine runbooks, and scale out migration using automation, robust logging, and checkpoint-based governance.

4
Handoff & Hardening

Deliver documentation and knowledge transfer; address post-cutover issues; finalize security posture and operational handoff.
